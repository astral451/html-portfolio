About Me

I'm a game developer and like the idea of using web based sites to provide information for our developers. They can then see data in a visual way that responds to their interaction with that site.

I've been creating games since 2001 and love it. I'm a Technical Artist, currently working at Undead Labs in Champaign Illinois.
